# SMB Share Interaction Count
## Purpose
Shows the count of SMB commands associated with
share interaction (READ, WRITE, DELETE, TRANS,
and IOCTL operations).

## Questions Answered
- How many share interactions happened over the range?
- What's the volumetric change in share interaction?
